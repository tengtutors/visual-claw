⚡ Avatar Editor — How to Use

Quick Start — Custom Sprite from ChatGPT

1
Copy the ChatGPT Prompt below, paste it into ChatGPT (works best with GPT-4o or GPT-4.5). Replace the placeholder description with your character.
2
Save the generated image as a PNG. The image should be a horizontal strip of 5 frames on a white or solid background.
3
Click ↑ Upload Sprite Strip in the left panel. Select your PNG.
4
In the upload dialog, preview the 5 detected frames. Adjust BG Tolerance if the background isn't fully removed. Give the character a name and click Add Character.
5
Your character appears in the left panel. Click it to select and preview the animation.
6
In the right panel, use the Map Size slider to adjust how large the character appears in the map. Try Small (0.6×) as a starting point.
7
Scroll down to Assign to Agent. Your connected bots appear as green chips — click one, then press Assign. The map updates immediately.
Tip: Frames 11–12 (Work loop) animate when your bot is working or processing a task. Frames 9–10 (Chill loop) animate when it is idle. Even 2-frame loops look great in motion.

Frame Layout

Your sprite strip must be a single PNG with frames arranged left-to-right. The editor auto-detects frame boundaries by scanning for gaps between columns.
Walk frames (1–8)
1 Front Idle
2 Front Walk A
3 Front Walk B
4 Back Idle
5 Back Walk A
6 Back Walk B
7 Side Walk A
8 Side Walk B
Idle animation loop (9–10) — plays when resting
9 Chill 1
10 Chill 2
Work animation loop (11–12) — plays when processing a task
11 Work 1
12 Work 2
Recommended size: 384×32 px (12 × 32×32 frames). Old 9-frame and 5-frame sprites still work automatically.

Troubleshooting

!
Frames not detected correctly? Increase BG Tolerance in the upload dialog. If frames merge together, re-generate with a plain white background.
!
Character too large in map? Select the sprite → right panel → Map Size → drag slider left or click Small. Changes apply immediately.
!
No agent chips showing? The map must be open and gateway connected first. Open the dashboard, wait for agents to appear, then return here.
!
Assignment not applying? After assigning, reload the map tab (Ctrl+R on the dashboard page).
